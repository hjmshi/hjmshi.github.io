I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in the Industrial Engineering and Management Sciences department at Northwestern University. My research interests are in numerical optimization and deep learning, with a focus on neural network training, stochastic optimization, noise-tolerant optimization, and derivative-free optimization. I've had the unique privilege of collaborating closely with industry, including Intel and Facebook, during my Ph.D. studies.

Previously, I have interned at Facebook under the AI System Co-Design Team as a research intern working on training properties and algorithms for deep learning-based recommendation systems.
