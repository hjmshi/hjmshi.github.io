Welcome to my website! My full name is Hao-Jun Michael Shi, although I normally go by Michael.

I am currently a research scientist at Meta in the AI and Systems Co-Design Training team, with an emphasis on the exploration (research) and implementation of scalable and distributed training optimization algorithms for deep learning. My recent focus has been on developing scalable adaptive gradient methods for training neural networks, including an efficient PyTorch implementation of the Distributed Shampoo optimizer.

In my free time, I like to eat good food, read interesting books, play basketball, root for UCLA sports teams (Go Bruins!), serve at my church, and spend time with my wife while attempting to entertain my cat Taro.
